What is StepMania?

StepMania is an open source rhythm game engine. It is primarily used to simulate the gameplay of Dance Dance Revolution, but it is capable of simulating other types of key-based rhythm games as well. StepMania has been developed since 2000, and has also been used as the engine of commercial arcade dance games such as In the Groove, and Pump it Up Pro and Infinity.
